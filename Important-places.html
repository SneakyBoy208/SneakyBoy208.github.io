Important places at EIDE

Library

The library is open from 8 AM to 5 PM. It offers a wide range of books, journals, and other resources for students and staff.



Laboratory

Various rooms in the E-Building are dedicated to laboratory work. It offers various equipment and resources for students and teachers to do experiences for educational purposes.



Gymnasium

The gymnasium, located in the Main Building, is open from 8 AM to 5 PM. It offers various sports equipment and facilities for students and teachers.
