Cafeteria

The cantine is open from 12:15 PM to 1:45 PM. It offers various meals, including a vegetarian and vegan option. It also provides desserts, entrees, beverages and snacks.



School Rules

There are several rules that students must follow at school. These include :


  • Respecting the school environment and the people around you.
  • Arriving on time for classes and activities.
  • Students are expected to dress appropriately for the establishment.
  • Not using electronic devices in classrooms and cantine.
  • Refraining from bullying, harassment, or any form of discrimination.
